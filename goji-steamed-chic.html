Goji Steamed Chicken

Easily one of the healthiest and easiest dish you can prepare.

This originates from the Hakka clan in which it's prepared for post-natal mothers due to the amount of nutrition they can acquire from this simple dish.

Of course, anybody can enjoy it, too!


Recipe and image provided by featsoffeasts.com

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken cut up to pieces (minus head, feet and butt)
  • Thumb size piece of ginger sliced to strips
  • 10 dried red dates
  • A generous handful of dried goji berries
  • A generous handful of dried goji berries
  • Water
  • Sesame oil
  • Salt
  • White Pepper

Instructions

  1. Rinse the goji berries and get rid of any impurities.
  2. Rinse the dates and score each of them. This brings out the flavour more.
  3. Heat a heavy based deep pot with a little sesame oil. Stir fry the ginger till fragrant.
  4. Add in the chicken and brown them all over.
  5. Add the ginger wine and let it boil in high heat to get rid of the alcohol.
  6. Top off with water
  7. Add the dates and goiji berries. Season with salt and white pepper.
  8. Let it come to a rolling boil. Skim off the impurities floating on top.
  9. Lower the heat to a simmer.
  10. Cover with lid, let it simmer for about an hour and a half.
